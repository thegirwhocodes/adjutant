    § 03 · One sentence. Three forms.

    "I'm going to JRTC at Fort Polk July 14 for five days, need to counsel SPC Garcia tomorrow, and want two days of leave when I get back."

    → One voice request. Three filled PDFs.
    001 DD-1351-2 + 002 DA-4856 + 003 DA-31

    No competing tool — GenAI.mil, CamoGPT, Ask Sage, Milnerva, EdgeRunner — does this. Adjutant's _infer_forms classifier reads action verbs and date phrases out of one sentence, then runs three independent schema-extraction passes in parallel.

    § 04 · The pipeline

    Five stages. All on the laptop. All offline.

    1. 001 Speak

      STT

      faster-whisper · small.en · int8

      Browser mic captured by AudioWorklet, downsampled to 16 kHz PCM16, streamed over WebSocket. Silero VAD gates the turn.

    2. 002 Recall

      RAG

      FAISS · MiniLM-L6 · threshold 0.35

      Curated corpus — AR 600-8-10, AR 27-10, AR 623-3, AR 735-5, JTR 2025. Below-threshold queries return zero chunks; the LLM is forced to refuse.

    3. 003 Reason

      LLM

      llama3.2 · 3B · Ollama · streaming

      Constrained-context prompt: must cite by section/paragraph. Sentence buffer flushes the moment a terminator hits, so the first audio leaves the server while sentence two is still generating.

    4. 004 Fill

      PDF

      pikepdf · reportlab overlay

      AcroForm fill is primary; reportlab text-on-coordinates handles DA-31's XFA-only PDF. GSA per-diem math runs in deterministic Python — never trusted to the LLM.

    5. 005 Speak

      TTS

      Kokoro-82M · ONNX · af_heart · 24 kHz

      A pre-rendered "checking the regs" cue plays within ~50 ms of end-of-speech, masking the LLM-prefill gap. Real reply chains in sentence-by-sentence behind it.

    § 05 · Architectural claims

    Three things that are not demo-day theater.

    a.

    Architecturally offline.

    Whisper, Llama, Kokoro, FAISS, MiniLM — all loaded from disk at startup. The wifi cable comes out before the demo starts. The badge flips to OFFLINE and nothing changes about the response.

    b.

    Architecturally cited.

    Every spoken summary ships with verbatim source quotes — section, paragraph, AR number. The citation panel populates before the audio starts. Hallucination probes are answered with a panel, not a promise.

    c.

    Architecturally refuses.

    A retrieval threshold of 0.35 means out-of-corpus questions return zero chunks. The LLM is then handed a hard refusal contract — it is structurally incapable of answering with what it doesn't have.

    § 06 · Eval

    Numbers, not adjectives.

    Run on a 2020 8 GB MacBook Air M1. Fixed corpus of seven ARs / pamphlets, a held-out probe set, and a 40-query out-of-corpus test for the refusal contract. We rerun and publish on the demo machine before judging.

    RETRIEVAL CORRECTNESS 92% n = 120 · top-3 chunk includes ground-truth
    REFUSAL CORRECTNESS 98% n = 40 · OOC queries below 0.35 sim score
    ESV → FAO · p50 1.2s end of speech → first audio · M1 · warm
    FORM-FILL ACCURACY 94% n = 60 · field-level F1 vs hand-graded
