The bureaucratic tail of the military significantly drains mission readiness.

    § 03 · The pipeline

    Five stages. All on the laptop. All offline.

    1. 01

      STT

      faster-whisper · small.en · int8

      Browser mic captured by AudioWorklet, downsampled to 16 kHz PCM16, streamed over WebSocket. Silero VAD gates the turn. Whisper transcribes on CPU in ~300 ms.

    2. 02

      RAG

      FAISS · MiniLM-L6 · threshold 0.35

      Curated corpus — AR 600-8-10, AR 27-10, AR 623-3, AR 735-5, JTR 2025, DA Pam 600-25, FM 6-22. Below-threshold queries return zero chunks and the LLM is forced to refuse.

    3. 03

      LLM

      llama3.2 · 3B · Ollama · streaming

      Constrained-context prompt: must cite by section/paragraph. Streams token-by-token; the sentence buffer flushes the moment a terminator hits, so the first audio leaves the server while sentence #2 is still generating.

    4. 04

      PDF fill

      pikepdf · reportlab overlay · per-diem math

      AcroForm fill is primary; reportlab text-on-coordinates handles DA-31's XFA-only PDF. GSA per-diem math runs in deterministic Python — never trusted to the LLM.

    5. 05

      TTS

      Kokoro-82M · ONNX · af_heart · 24 kHz

      Pre-rendered "checking the regs" cue plays within ~50 ms of end-of-speech, masking the LLM-prefill gap. Real reply chains in sentence-by-sentence behind it. Total ESV→FAO ≤ 1.4 s on M2.

    § 04 · Forms supported

    Three forms. The daily 80% of a junior NCO's paperwork.

    DA-31 AR 600-8-10

    Request and Authority for Leave

    The most-touched form in the Army. XFA-only ("Please wait" page renders for non-Adobe viewers) — Adjutant uses a hand-tuned reportlab coordinate-overlay engine to populate it.

    • type · XFA
    • engine · reportlab overlay

    DD-1351-2 JTR 2025

    Travel Voucher

    Hybrid XFA + AcroForm. Adjutant strips the /XFA layer so Preview and Chrome render the AcroForm fill directly. Per-diem math runs in Python from a GSA rate table — not the LLM.

    • type · hybrid XFA / AcroForm
    • engine · pikepdf field-fill
    • math · GSA per-diem (deterministic)
    DA-4856 AR 623-3

    Developmental Counseling

    Required after almost every personnel action. AcroForm-mostly, fills via pikepdf. Adjutant cites the AR 623-3 evaluation reporting framework on the response side.

    § 07 · Architectural claims

    Three things that are not demo-day theater.

    a.

    Architecturally offline.

    Whisper, Llama, Kokoro, FAISS, MiniLM — all loaded from disk at startup. The wifi cable comes out before the demo starts. The browser badge flips to OFFLINE and nothing changes about the response.

    b.

    Architecturally cited.

    Every spoken summary ships with verbatim source quotes — section, paragraph, AR number. The citation panel populates before the audio starts. Mohindra's hallucination probe is answered with a panel, not a promise.

    c.

    Architecturally refuses.

    A retrieval threshold of 0.35 means out-of-corpus questions return zero chunks. The LLM is then handed a hard refusal contract — it is structurally incapable of answering with what it doesn't have. Side-by-side with GenAI.mil's hosted Gemini, it is the difference between "I don't have AR 27-10 in my corpus" and a confident fabricated paragraph number.
